A smartwatch is a wearable device that combines the functionality of a traditional watch with features commonly found in smartphones. It typically has a touchscreen display and can connect to your phone via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi. Smartwatches allow you to check notifications, track fitness metrics like steps, heart rate, and sleep patterns, and control music playback. Some models support voice commands, GPS, and even mobile payments. They often come with customizable watch faces, fitness apps, and compatibility with both iOS and Android devices, making them versatile and convenient gadgets.
